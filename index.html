Lift Curve Generator

This is a basic web application written in HTML, CSS and Javascript. Once the variables in the form below have been input and the generate button selected, a lift coefficient vs angle of attack graph will be generated. This application is intended as an educational tool, to easily demonstrate the affect of a wing's aspect ratio, Mach number and efficiency factor on the resultant lift coefficient at different angles of attack. Developed by Robert Duckham, an Aircraft Engineering student at UWS.
